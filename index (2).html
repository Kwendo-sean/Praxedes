Imani, Today marks three months of us, and I couldn’t be more grateful to have you in my life. I know last night hurt, and I want to say this clearly: I’m sorry. I’m not perfect, but I’m growing — and I want to grow with you. If you’ll let me, I’ll learn how to love you better, louder, and softer in all the right ways. Happy 3 months, baby. I love you, and I’m sorry.
